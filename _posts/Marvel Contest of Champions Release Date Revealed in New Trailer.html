Marvel Contest of Champions Release Date Revealed in New Trailer

Greetings, true believers! Are you ready for some super good news? Kabam’s upcoming comic book brawler has a new trailer, and that trailer has a big reveal. While the exact day hasn’t been pinned down yet, you can expect Marvel Contest of Champions release date to happen this December.

The game has recently soft-launched in Denmark, and those who’ve played it are quickly drawing comparisons to the mobile version of Injustice: Gods Among Us (aka, the portable DC punch’em’up). It’s not a clone?by any stretch, but it sounds like they’re cut from a similar cloth.

If you’re at the New York Comic-Con this weekend, be sure to check it out at the Marvel booth (#1354).

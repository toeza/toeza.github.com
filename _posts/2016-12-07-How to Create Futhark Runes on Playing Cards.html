Community Q&A

Learn how to create Futhark Runes on ordinary playing cards, and bring luck to all of your future card endeavors.

Steps

  1. 1 Purchase a deck of 52 playing cards (preferably the jumbo style) and a sharpie. Consult a book or website on tarot/playing card conversions and meaning and Rune symbols and meanings.
  2. 2 Research tarot card meanings which correspond with a specific Rune. This is so your Runes are in harmony with the card it is on. You won't be using the Runes to interpret the card; it is just for harmonies sake. You don't want to put a positive rune on a negative meaning card.
  3. 3 After you have determined the card for a specific Rune, use your sharpie and write the Rune in the upper right hand corner, top and bottom. Do this for all the Runes.
  4. 4 Try finding a card that represents yourself as a cover card. Write your magical name (if you have one) or just your name on the top and bottom of card. This card represents you. Set it at the top of the area you are doing Rune casting on.
  5. 5 Discard extra cards.
  6. 6 Consecrate them according to your own style. Enjoy your new cards!

How to Play Bond Bond

Community Q&A

Bond Bond is a fun card game suitable for all ages.It's a good idea to play on a rainy day or just pass some time and it only needs some players and a deck of cards.Gather some friends read this article if you don't know how to play.

Steps

  1. 1 Make sure you have at least 2-3 people.
  2. 2 Deal the cards so that there's no more, and everyone has the same amount of cards.
  3. 3 Make a big circle for all the people to fit in and sit comfortably for the game to start smoothly.
  4. 4 Tell everybody to divide their cards into two piles,one for the number ones and the second pile must be for the other cards example:king, joker and queen.
  5. 5 Be sure that every player cannot see their cards and place them backwards and throw the cards placed backwards.
    • Every player should get their turn. When a turn comes,the player should throw his/her number card.
  6. 6 When the player throws the exact same card which was thrown by the person before your turn,you will get all the cards thrown by all players!
  7. 7 When the player has only one card and wishes to have more.Now the other cards will come in. These cards are worth very much.Each card has a specific amount of number cards. (The amounts are listed in the tips section)
  8. 8 Give this card to a player and the player will give you the amount of number cards the card is worth.
  9. 9 Once again, if a player throws the same card,the player will get the number cards and the other card the person used to take cards.
  10. 10 Keep doing this until the players number cards and other cards are finished.
